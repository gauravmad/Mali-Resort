    About Us

    At Samarth Krupa Agro Tourism Farm, we strive to offer our guests a truly unique and unforgettable experience. Whether you're interested in learning about sustainable farming practices, connecting with nature, or just relaxing and unwinding in a picturesque setting, our farm has something for everyone.
    Explore the beauty of our 15-acre organic farm, where you can spend as much time as you like visiting the various elements of organic farming. Meet producers on their farms, in their living environments, amid their fields, animals, buildings, and the rural landscapes. Our farm is home to acres of fruit, coconut trees, shrubs, and wildlife, providing a serene and unspoiled natural environment for anyone looking for a soothing and relaxing holiday away from the distractions of the world
    In addition to our authentic agro-tourism experiences, we offer a range of exciting activities for guests, including archery, tractor riding, and swimming in our refreshing pools. Whether you're looking for adventure or relaxation, there's something for everyone at our farm.
