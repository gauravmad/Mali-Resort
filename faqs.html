    Frequently Asked Questions

    Agro tourism, also known as agritourism or farm tourism, is a form of tourism that involves visiting agricultural areas, farms, or rural environments to experience and participate in agricultural activities. It offers travelers the opportunity to learn about farming practices, engage in farm activities, and enjoy the rural lifestyle.


    Agro tourism benefits both tourists and local communities. For tourists, it provides a unique and educational experience, a chance to connect with nature, and an understanding of farming and rural life. Local communities benefit from increased income, job opportunities, and the preservation of cultural heritage and agricultural traditions.


    Yes, agro tourism is suitable for children. It provides an excellent opportunity for kids to learn about farming, animals, and nature. However, always ensure that the activities are safe and suitable for their age.
