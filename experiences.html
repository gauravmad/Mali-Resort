    Experiences

    Bullock Cart Ride

    The bullock cart ride allows guests to enjoy the natural beauty of the surroundings at a leisurely place.

    Archery

    Archery is primarily a sport or activity rather than a mode of transportation, resorts may offer archery experiences for their guests.

    Fishing

    The resort would have designated fishing spots, such as lakes, ponds, rivers, or even private fishing docks. These spots would be carefully chosen to offer a serene and picturesque setting for guests to enjoy their fishing experience.

    Tractor Ride

    he tractor ride allows guests to explore the farm or agricultural estate, providing a unique opportunity to get up close and personal with the working farm environment. It may include driving through fields or other areas where various crops are grown.

    Shooting

    Resorts offering shooting experiences may have experienced instructors or shooting coaches available to provide guidance and assistance to guests. These professionals can offer valuable tips, correct shooting techniques, and help guests improve their skills and accuracy.

    Artificial Waterfall

    Artificial waterfalls in resorts are often designed to be large-scale and visually impressive. They may have multiple tiers or cascades, creating a dramatic and majestic focal point within the resort's landscape.
